Frequently Asked Questions

1. How will I get back the money?
Each month, based on the repayment from the borrowers, amount will be accrued in your wallet which you shall be able to see in your dashboard. You can withdraw your money to your bank account by clicking on the withdraw button.
2. What is the money being used for?
The money is being used to purchase the solar homelighting system and the rural household is given the convenience of repayment in monthly instalments.
3. Am i paying the whole amount for the purchase of the product?
No, the rural household shall atleast contribute 50% of the cost of the product at the time of purchase.
4. What is the cost of the product?
The cost of the product is Rs. 5000.
5. Will all households require 50% of the product cost as assistance at the beginning?
No, the requirements of the households may vary from 1% of the product cost to 50% of product cost.
6. Who is conducting due diligence?
The field team of the Lytyfy will conduct the diligence and upload the list of eligible customers on the portal.
7. Is there a guarantee that my investment will be repaid?
No, there is no guarantee that the amount shall be repaid. However, results in the microfinance industry per se are very positive and we shall take utmost care to ensure that the social investors shall get their investment back in due course of time.
8. Am I giving a donation?
No, you are not giving a donation.
9. Why am I getting an interest of 0.60% per month whereas the rural customer is charged 2% per month?
We are utilising the difference for conducting due diligence, collection of repayments and other processes.
10. What kind of interest rate calculations are we doing, simple or compound?
We are using simple interest for all our calculations.
11. Will i be informed about the monthly progress of my investment?
Yes, at any point of time you can see the performance of your investment in your dash board. The variations in the expected monthly returns shall also be visible.
