Lytyfy facilitates end user financing for rural customers who are willing to adopt clean energy products but are restricted due to lack of capital. Rural customers who are interested in adopting solar technology are identified after conducting due diligence. The amount of loan required by a rural customer and the number of installments in which the rural customer can repay the loan are established based on the various parameters deduced using the due diligence process.
    The profiles of the rural customers who request for installment option are put up on Lytyfy and the money raised is used by the rural customer for purchasing solar home lighting systems which is then installed in their households. The rural customers then returns the money to the social investor in monthly installments. The interest that will be charged to the rural customer will be 2.00% per month. The interest rate that shall be provided to the social investor will be 0.60% per month. The difference between the two interest rates will be utilised for facilitating the transactions and the whole process.
